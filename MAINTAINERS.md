## Appointment

A maintainer candidate should demonstrate sustained contributions, accurate
handling of fact and inference, respect for security and evidence boundaries,
and capacity to review and maintain accepted work. Appointment requires a
governance pull request naming responsibilities and repository permissions.

## Inactivity And Succession

A maintainer who expects to be unavailable should document delegation where
practical. If the sole maintainer is inactive, contributors may continue to
fork under Apache-2.0, but no contributor should claim release or repository
authority they do not possess. A successor must be established through a
verifiable repository-owner action and a governance update.

## Release Authority

Only maintainers explicitly authorized for the protected `release` and
`npm-publish` environments may ship releases. Repository write access does not
implicitly grant release authority.
